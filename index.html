Wonders Of Philippines


If tropical islands, beautiful landscapes, and unending adventures are on your bucket list for a holiday trip, then you have found a gem of a destination with the Philippines.

The Philippines, with over 7,000 islands, offers a blend of nature, culture, and fun. Its geographical features provide natural attractions in Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. Attractions include beautiful beaches, turquoise waters, cone volcano, primate, surfing spots, and Spanish fortresses.



El Nido in Palawan is known for its paradise-like islands and lagoons, but a 45-minute ride from its main town offers Nacpan Beach, a 4-kilometer shoreline with golden sand and palm trees. After visiting coves, white-sand beaches, limestone cliffs, and other tourist spots, visit the Twin Beaches of El Nido.

Intramuros, a 64-hectare Spanish fortress in Manila, is a top landmark and Instagrammable area. Despite being destroyed by World War II, its ruins remain today. The area features universities, churches, and historical sites like the Manila Cathedral and San Agustin Church, making it an ideal Philippines staycation spot.

The Chocolate Hills of Bohol, a UNESCO World Heritage Natural Monument, are a unique landscape with 1,268 cone-shaped hills that turn chocolatey brown during the dry season. These limestone hills range from 30 to 120 meters in height and are a must-see for Bohol trips. Other attractions include Loboc river cruises, Balicasag Island diving, Panglao Island tours, Philippine Tarsier Sanctuary visits, and night-time visits to the Abatan River.
